# Business Insights

## Revenue Insights
- A small percentage of customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
- Sales are concentrated in a few high-performing regions.

## Product Insights
- Certain genres consistently outperform others in revenue.
- A limited set of artists drive the majority of track sales.

## Customer Insights
- Repeat customers have higher average invoice values.
- Region-specific preferences exist for music genres.

## Business Takeaway
The analysis highlights opportunities for targeted marketing,
inventory optimization, and region-focused promotional strategies.
